Trip to Bali

Story by Shafkat Reaz

Broken Beach, Bali

Bali is a paradise for budget backpackers and one of the most popular travel hub in Indonesia for travelers, yoga lovers, honeymooners, and digital nomads alike.

Visiting Bali has become so cliched these days with so many travelers just vising there for expensive spa retreats. However, I still find the island to be exquisitely beautiful, budget friendly, and full of things to do. The countryside is beautiful, the beaches (outside of Kuta) are perfect frr your Instagram, there is great hiking, friendly locals, world-class dining, and most importantly it’s inexpensive.

I spent a very inadequate amount of 5 days exploring the island with my wife and am eagerly waiting to go back and explore it again.

The trick to traveling Bali is to get away from Ubud and Kuta. Once you do so, you’ll find a much calmer, cheaper, and less-busy island worth exploring where the touts don’t bother you and the empty beaches are yours to enjoy.

Amongst MANY other things to do in Bali, I found the following list of to-dos to be totally unavoidable:

  1. Hike Mount Batur
  2. Take a trip to Nusa Penida
    • Broken Beach
    • Kelinking Beach
    • Angel Billabong
    • Crystal Bay
  3. Scuba Diving and snorkelling
  4. Visit Uluwatu Temple
  5. Roam around Bali by renting scooters
  6. Visit the different type of beaches
  7. Bali swing
  8. Ubud Rice Fields
